Bio & Research Interest

Takatso Sibanda

Doctoral Candidate, Community Psychology

Michigan State University, Department of Psychology

  • LinkedIn
  • Email

    • I am a community-engaged researcher and community psychologist with extensive experience across health and social service sectors. My work is grounded in community-centered, rights-based approaches to wellness promotion that elevate local insight and lived experience in the design, implementation, and evaluation of well-being initiatives. I have developed strong partnerships with marginalized communities, civil society organizations, government ministries, healthcare providers, and interdisciplinary teams throughout my career.
